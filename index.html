Liam Noyce is a versatile, attack-minded winger from Denver, Colorado, currently contracted with CF Riba-Roja in Valencia, Spain. His footballing education spans five countries — training and competing with academy sides in Germany and Austria through The TalentProjekt, at the prestigious MIC Cup in Barcelona, with São Paulo FC in Brazil, and against Boca Juniors and River Plate in Argentina. He combines technical quality on the ball with tactical discipline, pace, and an understanding of the European style of play. Off the pitch, he brings discipline, professionalism, and the ability to communicate — fluent in English, conversational in Spanish and German (B1).
